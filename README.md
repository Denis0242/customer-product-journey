# 🚀 Executive Summary


Customer Product Analytics Dashboard is an end-to-end Product Data Science project that analyzes 
the full customer lifecycle — from discovery and conversion to retention, profitability, and churn prediction.
The platform integrates customer analytics, product performance, unit economics, growth metrics, and
predictive insights into a single interactive analytics system built using Python, Streamlit, Pandas, and Plotly.
This project demonstrates how data science and product analytics transform operational data into actionable 
business intelligence for product, growth, and strategy teams.

# 📈 Business Impact
_This analytics platform enables product and growth teams to:_
- Identify high-value customer segments using lifecycle analysis
- Detect customers at risk of churn before revenue is lost
- Analyze product profitability and category performance
- Monitor acquisition efficiency using CAC and LTV metrics
- Diagnose conversion funnel drop-offs in the customer journey
- Support data-driven product and marketing decisions

The system transforms raw operational data into actionable insights that improve revenue, retention,
and product strategy.

# 📊 Dashboard Modules

**The Streamlit dashboard contains 7 analytical modules designed to answer critical business questions**.

# 1️⃣ Executive Overview
_High-level business performance metrics including:_
- Total revenue
- Total customers
- Active customers
- Average order value (AOV)
- Repeat purchase rate
- Gross margin

_Purpose:_
Quick snapshot of overall business health and performance.

---
# 2️⃣ Customer Journey Analysis
_Customer funnel analysis across digital touchpoints._

Page View → Product View → Add to Cart → Checkout → Purchase

_Insights include:_
- Funnel conversion rates
- Stage drop-off analysis
- Customer behavioral patterns

_Purpose:_
Identify friction points in the purchase journey.

# 3️⃣ Product Performance Analysis

_Product catalog evaluation including:_
- Revenue by category
- Top-performing products
- Product lifecycle stages
- Category profitability

_Purpose:_
Identify which products drive revenue and margins.

---
# 4️⃣ Unit Economics

_Financial sustainability analysis including:_
- Revenue vs profit
- Gross margin
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
- LTV:CAC ratio

_Purpose:_
Evaluate whether growth is profitable and scalable.

---
# 5️⃣ Growth Metrics
_Business expansion analysis including:_
- Month-over-month revenue growth
- Customer acquisition trends
- Cohort retention analysis
- Payback period

_Purpose:_
Measure growth efficiency and retention performance.

# 6️⃣ Customer Experience (3D Analysis)
_Multi-dimensional customer behavior visualization using:_
- Engagement
- Purchases
- Total spend

_Purpose:_
Understand high-value vs low-value customer segments.

---
# 7️⃣ Predictive Analytics
_Forward-looking insights including:_
- Customer Lifetime Value estimation
- Churn risk prediction
- High-value customer identification

_Purpose:_
Move from descriptive analytics to predictive decision support.
